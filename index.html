About

„Vid“ is a simple bookmarklet that styles a page for comfortable viewing of non-fullscreen video. It allows you to choose a video object and „extracts“ it, i.e.

  • removes everything from the page except the chosen object;
  • centers the object on the page;
  • makes page background black.

How to use

Drag this button Vid to the bookmarks bar in your browser. A new bookmark should appear.

On a page with a video activate the bookmarklet by clicking the bookmark and then:

  1. Hover cursor over a page block containing a video object. Bookmarklet will

    • show a red outline around the block;
    • automatically locate the first object within the block and highlight it with yellow.

  2. If the yellow-highlighted object is what you need, press Enter or left-click within the red-outlined block to confirm your choice (don't click the object itself as the bookmarklet cannot catch it). Otherwise, you can use your mouse/touchpad/etc. again to select a smaller page block containing just the video you want. Alternatively, you can iterate objects within the red-onlined block using Space or Tab (hold Shift to reverse).

At any time, you can cancel by pressing Esc.

Warning: extraction may cause a loss of video position and already downloaded data.

Further Notes

Tested in Firefox 3.6. If the bookmarket does not work in your browser, write an issue.
