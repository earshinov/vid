„Vid“ is a simple bookmarklet that styles a page for comfortable viewing of non-fullscreen video. It allows you to choose a video object and then

  1. removes everything from the page except the chosen object;
  2. centers the object on the page;
  3. makes page background black.

How to use:

  1. Drag this button Vid to the bookmarks bar in your browser. A new bookmark should appear.
  2. On a page with a video activate the bookmarklet by clicking the bookmark you added at the previous step. Using your mouse/touchpad/etc. choose a page block containing a single video object (bookmarklet will help you showind red outline around the active block). Confirm your choice with left click or cancel by pressing Esc.

Notes:

  • When applying the bookmarklet, you may lose video position and already downloaded data.

Tested in Firefox 3.6.
